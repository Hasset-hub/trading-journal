CSV import accepts columns: symbol, direction, entryDate, entryPrice, quantity, stopLoss, takeProfit, exitDate, exitPrice, commission, fees, setup, tags. Download the template for the exact format. Existing trades are kept; imported rows are added.

About

A personal trading journal modeled after the workflows of professional traders. Tracks R-multiples (Van Tharp), setup quality (Minervini/Raschke), psychology (Douglas), MAE/MFE excursions, and the full suite of performance statistics. All data is stored locally in your browser.
